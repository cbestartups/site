Coimbatore

Known as the Manchester of South India, Coimbatore is an industrial city and home to a large number of global enterprises and famous educational institutions. Fostering entrepreneurship has been a culture of Coimbatore, the second largest city in the state of Tamil Nadu. Textiles, Pumps, Wet Grinders, Heavy machineries, Information Technology and Aerospace are the most popular industrial verticals in Coimbatore with over 10000+ large enterprises operating in these spaces. Coimbatore is also known for its serene climate and a rich cultural diversity.
